ABOUT US

Resonance was birthed in 2016 from a keen desire to influence positive change and to be an agent for that change we need in our world today!

Our gaze is set on the youths of a nation; a generation of youth hungry for attention, craving fun and wanting to be in the cool clique. Our mandate is to help them imbibe the right culture, harness their skills and drive them to assert themselves positively while, leaving them with mentorship that will help them chose the right kind of flow and set a standard through music, art, poetry, dance even business.

PROJECT

This season, Resonance in collaboration with “The Gold Badge” (TGB - An organization dedicated to, amongst others, raising awareness of the values through which a great society is built.), we choose to spread love through our end of the year Christmas project tagged; “JOY OF CHRISTMAS”. This project, just as the name implies, will restore the joy of Christmas to the homes of selected 30 families as we set about providing for their Christmas needs and even returning the long old tradition of sharing and celebrating the birth of our Christ through love. Feeding these family depends on the generous donations from individuals and organizations to help restore the joy of Christmas to the inside cities and small communities of Lagos state. We invite you to join us by making charitable donations and sponsorship to the cause.
